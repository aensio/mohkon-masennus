Event Briefing · Kesäkisa 2026

The Info

You are invited to a functional fitness summer event organised by Anttix and Esax at the Tervaruukki beach in Ilomantsi on Saturday 30.05.2026 at 11:00. The guest list is built from Antti and Esa's circle of friends.

After registration we'll split the group into teams of three or four. We (Anttix & Esax) will balance teams so everyone gets a fair challenge but the gaps stay small. The core idea: the joy of moving, time together with our own crew, and above all — enjoying Ilomantsi's untouched and beautiful nature.

01 / Schedule

⏱Event flow

  • 11.00Arrival on site — be on time!
  • 11.15Race briefing & team draw
  • 11.30Start. Two-part race: trail run + functional stations on the beach
  • 16.00Wrap-up
02 / Kit

🎽What to wear

  • Race kit Appropriate clothing + running shoes. On the beach: music speakers and — at minimum — a "Yyteri" vibe.
  • Evening & lodging Own bed linen, own food, and something cold to drink around the fire.
03 / Food

🍕Catering

Option to order pizza either to the beach or to the cabin — we'll sort it on the spot.

04 / Lodging

🏕Evening at the cabin

After the race, the evening peaks at Pirttijärven Erän Maja: sauna, swim in the rapids, sleeping quarters. 8 beds + 10 mattresses. Drive there in your own cars. There's a kota and grilling on site — and the team masters may have a small treat in store.

📍Venue
